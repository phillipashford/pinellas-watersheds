Find parks within one mile of your current location with MapLibre's geolocation service. The map will then dynamically query DHS.gov Open Data Portal for protected areas (PADUS) within one mile of your location. The map then filters for publicly open areas and uses Turf.js to buffer and intersect these areas with your location. The map displays the results and shows a list in the below section.
